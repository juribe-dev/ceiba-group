How it works

Up and running in 20 minutes.
Automated forever after.

We set everything up for you. Your team does one simple thing — the rest is automatic.

1

We set you up

We connect your Google Business Profile and configure your automated review flow. Takes us about 20 minutes — zero work for you.

2

Staff submits name + number

After a visit, your team types the customer's name and phone into a simple form. That's it — 30 seconds max.

3

Customer gets a friendly text

One hour later, they get a personalized SMS with a direct link to your Google review page. No searching required.

4

Reviews roll in automatically

If they don't respond, we send one polite follow-up after 3 days. You watch your rating climb without doing anything extra.
